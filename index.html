What Is Loch’d In?

What Is Loch’d In?

Loch’d In is a 10-hour hackathon for 15–18 year olds. Team up, make something cool (apps, websites, or games), then show it off on stage. The theme is “Myths & Makers,” but it’s a prompt — not a restriction.

Is this all free?

Absolutely!

Thanks to our sponsors we are able to make this event free to attend.

Food, drinks, snacks, and swag are provided.

Just bring a laptop and charger.

Tell us about allergens or dietary requirements during sign-up and we’ll plan accordingly.

Who can attend?

All 15–18 year olds. Coders, designers, artists and vibecoders are all welcome.

You can be in teams up to 4 and you will be able to form teams at the event.
